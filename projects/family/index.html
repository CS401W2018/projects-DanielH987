The Olympian Gods
Zeus Hera Poseidon Athena Apollo
  • Zeus is the king of the gods and rules the sky.
  • Poseidon controls the seas and is known for his trident.
  • Hera is the queen of the gods and the goddess of marriage.

Zeus: King of the Gods

Zeus is the ruler of Mount Olympus and the god of thunder. Known for his mighty lightning bolt, he governs the heavens and maintains order among the gods and mortals.

Poseidon: God of the Sea

Poseidon, the brother of Zeus, commands the ocean and is responsible for storms and earthquakes. Sailors prayed to him for safe voyages.

Hera: Queen of the Gods

Hera is the goddess of marriage and family, known for her jealousy and devotion to maintaining the sanctity of marriage. She is both a powerful and protective deity.
