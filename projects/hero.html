Rubik's Cube Hero: Feliks Zemdegs

Interests Page | Hero Page

Feliks Zemdegs is a famous Rubik's Cube solver who has set many world records. His skills and methods have inspired many people to try speedcubing.

World Records
3x3x3 Cube: Multiple records under 5 seconds
4x4x4 Cube: Numerous record-breaking solves
One-Handed Solves: Known for his incredible speed
Achievements
Two-time World Cube Association Champion
Creator of popular speedcubing tutorials
Inspirational figure in the Rubik's Cube community
