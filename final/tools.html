Tools You'll Want in Your Toolbox

Every CS student needs a few go-to tools to survive the grind. These are the most useful ones BYUH students have recommended.

  • VS Code: Lightweight and powerful code editor. Great extensions!
  • GitHub: Collaborate with others, manage versions, and keep backups.
  • LeetCode: Practice coding problems, especially useful for CS 250 and interviews.
  • Notion: Great for notes, planning, and organizing coursework.

Learning these early will save you a lot of stress later. Be sure to try each tool and find what works for your style.
